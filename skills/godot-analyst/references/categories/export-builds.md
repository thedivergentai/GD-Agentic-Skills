# Anara Rubric: Export Builds Excellence Metrics

## Evolutionary Benchmark: Production Readiness

| Criterion | Visionary Excellence (+20 Pts) | Architectural Slop (-10 Pts) | Expert Analysis (Anara's Insight) |
|:----------|:-------------------------------|:-----------------------------|:-----------------------------------|
| Pipeline Maturity | Automated CI/CD with version-control integration. | Manual 'Export Project' clicks for every release. | Automation is the shield against error. |
| Security | PCK encryption and script obfuscation for release. | Shipping with debug-symbols and source-code visible. | Protect the vision from the curious. |
| Resource Stripping | Target-platform texture compression presets. | Desktop-res assets included in mobile builds. | Weight is the enemy of the download. |
