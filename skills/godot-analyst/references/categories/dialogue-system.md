# Anara Rubric: Dialogue System Excellence Metrics

## Evolutionary Benchmark: Gameplay Purity

| Criterion | Visionary Excellence (+20 Pts) | Architectural Slop (-10 Pts) | Expert Analysis (Anara's Insight) |
|:----------|:-------------------------------|:-----------------------------|:-----------------------------------|
| Logic Isolation | Stateless narrative nodes with external variable sync. | Dialogue-logic hardcoded into individual NPC scripts. | The story is a stream; the NPC is the faucet. |
| Visual Fidelity | RichTextLabel mastery with custom BBCode effects. | Flat-text labels with no emphasis or character. | Words have weight; show them. |
| Event Integration | Gameplay-trigger hooks embedded in dialogue trees. | Manually checking global flags after every choice. | Interaction is the heart of the story. |


### 🌟 Visionary's Blueprint
Score for 'Flow'. Narrative is the connection to the vision.
