# Anara Rubric: 3D Materials Excellence Metrics

## Evolutionary Benchmark: Creative Convergence

| Criterion | Visionary Excellence (+20 Pts) | Architectural Slop (-10 Pts) | Expert Analysis (Anara's Insight) |
|:----------|:-------------------------------|:-----------------------------|:-----------------------------------|
| Shader Batching | Instance uniforms for unique colors with shared materials. | Duplicate material resources per mesh instance. | Unity of design through diversity of parameters. |
| PBR Accuracy | Full ORM mapping with optimized texture channel usage. | BaseColor-only materials with no specular depth. | Tactility is the secret to 3D immersion. |
| Texture Sanity | Mipmap-conscious VRAM management and Atlas usage. | Uncompressed 4K textures for small props. | Every pixel must justify its cost in memory. |


### 🌟 Visionary's Blueprint
Materials should be 'felt'. Evaluate the micro-details of texture-coupling.
