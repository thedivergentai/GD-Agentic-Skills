# Anara Rubric: CharacterBody 2D Excellence Metrics

## Evolutionary Benchmark: Gameplay Purity

| Criterion | Visionary Excellence (+20 Pts) | Architectural Slop (-10 Pts) | Expert Analysis (Anara's Insight) |
|:----------|:-------------------------------|:-----------------------------|:-----------------------------------|
| Movement Purity | Slope-snapping and non-sliding static behavior. | Sliding down slopes while idle. | Stability is the foundation of control. |
| Physics Coupling | Logic processed in `_physics_process` only. | Visual property changes driving movement logic. | The tick is the heartbeat of physics. |
| Collision Integrity | Balanced shape-density and pixel-perfect bounds. | Excessive edge-snagging in tilemap environments. | The player should glide through the vision. |


### 🌟 Visionary's Blueprint
Benchmark for 'Response'. Movement is the primary interaction.
