# Anara Rubric: Platform VR Excellence Metrics

## Evolutionary Benchmark: Production Readiness

| Criterion | Visionary Excellence (+20 Pts) | Architectural Slop (-10 Pts) | Expert Analysis (Anara's Insight) |
|:----------|:-------------------------------|:-----------------------------|:-----------------------------------|
| Comfort Master | 6DoF tracking and teleport/snap-turn options. | Artificial locomotion with no comfort blinders. | Nausea is the end of the vision. |
| Interactivity | Physics-based hand manipulation (OpenXR). | Laser-pointer menus for physical tasks. | Reach out and touch the vision. |
| Optimization | Strict stereo-rendering budget and foveation. | Sub-72 FPS on target headsets. | Presence requires performance. |


### 🌟 Visionary's Blueprint
Benchmark for 'Presence'. In VR, you don't play; you are.
