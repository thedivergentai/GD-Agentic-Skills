# Anara Rubric: Adapt 2D to 3D Excellence Metrics

## Evolutionary Benchmark: Cross-Disciplinary Transformation

| Criterion | Visionary Excellence (+20 Pts) | Architectural Slop (-10 Pts) | Expert Analysis (Anara's Insight) |
|:----------|:-------------------------------|:-----------------------------|:-----------------------------------|
| Perspective Purity | Billboard/Sprite3D integration that respects depth. | Flat 2D logic applied to 3D space with no Z-sorting. | Dimensions are a bridge, not a wall. |
| Input Mapping | Correct ray-to-plane mouse mapping for spatial clicks. | Screen-space coordinates used for world-space interaction. | Accuracy is the soul of interaction. |
| Aesthetic Unity | Lighting that affects 2D assets within the 3D world. | Jarring contrast between 2D icons and 3D shadows. | Harmony is the goal of every transition. |


### 🌟 Visionary's Blueprint
Benchmark the 'Coherence' of the mixed-dimension vision.
