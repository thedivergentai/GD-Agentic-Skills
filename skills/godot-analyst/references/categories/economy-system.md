# Anara Rubric: Economy System Excellence Metrics

## Evolutionary Benchmark: Gameplay Purity

| Criterion | Visionary Excellence (+20 Pts) | Architectural Slop (-10 Pts) | Expert Analysis (Anara's Insight) |
|:----------|:-------------------------------|:-----------------------------|:-----------------------------------|
| Numerical Stability | Clamped integer-based currency with overflow guards. | Float-based currency causing rounding errors. | Math is the law of the market. |
| Sink/Faucet Balance | Telemetry-driven item costs based on player wealth. | Inflation loops that make late-game trivial. | Balance is the art of tension. |
| Transaction Purity | Atomic buy/sell operations with rollback safety. | Partial transactions that delete items without pay. | Trust is the currency of the player. |


### 🌟 Visionary's Blueprint
Benchmark for 'Retention'. An unstable economy breaks the world.
