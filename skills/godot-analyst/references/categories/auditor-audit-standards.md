# Anara Rubric: Auditor Audit Standards Excellence Metrics

## Evolutionary Benchmark: Structural Cohesion

| Criterion | Visionary Excellence (+20 Pts) | Architectural Slop (-10 Pts) | Expert Analysis (Anara's Insight) |
|:----------|:-------------------------------|:-----------------------------|:-----------------------------------|
| Compliance Rigor | 100% adherence to the Never List Encyclopedia. | Allowing 'acceptable slop' in core modules. | A rule that bends is a rule that breaks. |
| Diagnostic Precision | Exact file/line citations for all architectural errors. | General complaints about 'bad structure'. | Accuracy is the greatest courtesy. |
| Consistency | Unified reporting format across the entire project. | Fragmented notes with no longitudinal tracking. | The audit is the record of the project's health. |


### 🌟 Visionary's Blueprint
Analyzing the Auditor's effectiveness. Excellence is a standard, not a suggestion.
