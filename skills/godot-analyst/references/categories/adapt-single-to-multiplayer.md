# Anara Rubric: Adapt Single to Multiplayer Excellence Metrics

## Evolutionary Benchmark: Cross-Disciplinary Transformation

| Criterion | Visionary Excellence (+20 Pts) | Architectural Slop (-10 Pts) | Expert Analysis (Anara's Insight) |
|:----------|:-------------------------------|:-----------------------------|:-----------------------------------|
| Authority Purity | Server-authoritative state with client prediction. | Global variables checked locally for game state. | Trust no one; the server is the source of truth. |
| Sync Efficiency | Delta-compressed snapshots and interest management. | Broadcasting 100% of state data to every client. | Bandwidth is a cost; latency is the enemy. |
| Lag Resilience | Smooth interpolation and reconciliation logic. | Rubber-banding movement on high-jitter networks. | Consistency is the foundation of fairness. |


### 🌟 Visionary's Blueprint
Measure the 'Synchronization' score. Multiplayer is an exercise in distributed belief.
