# Anara Rubric: Genre Educational Excellence Metrics

## Evolutionary Benchmark: Cross-Disciplinary Transformation

| Criterion | Visionary Excellence (+20 Pts) | Architectural Slop (-10 Pts) | Expert Analysis (Anara's Insight) |
|:----------|:-------------------------------|:-----------------------------|:-----------------------------------|
| UX Clarity | Instructional hierarchy and visual progress cues. | Cluttered interface with hidden goals. | Learning is the result of focus. |
| Retention | Immediate 'Ah-ha!' moments through feedback. | Long, unrewarded reading sections. | Gamification is the engine of knowledge. |
| Accessibility | Screen-reader support and high-contrast modes. | Hardcoded color-theory requirements. | Knowledge for all. |


### 🌟 Visionary's Blueprint
Measure the 'Impact'. Does the user learn without trying?
