# Anara Rubric: Mechanic Secrets Excellence Metrics

## Evolutionary Benchmark: Progressive Loops

| Criterion | Visionary Excellence (+20 Pts) | Architectural Slop (-10 Pts) | Expert Analysis (Anara's Insight) |
|:----------|:-------------------------------|:-----------------------------|:-----------------------------------|
| Discovery | Multi-layered hints and environmental cues. | Secrets found by random wall-bumping only. | A secret is a conversation. |
| Obfuscation | Encrypted or hidden flags in the save-data. | Plain-text 'SecretFound = true' in JSON. | The reveal is the reward. |
| Impact | Unique mechanical shifts for secret discoveries. | Generic +10 points for finding the hidden area. | A secret should change the game. |


### 🌟 Visionary's Blueprint
Evaluate the 'Mystery'. If it's too easy, it's not a secret.
