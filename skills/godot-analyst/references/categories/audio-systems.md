# Anara Rubric: Audio Systems Excellence Metrics

## Evolutionary Benchmark: Creative Convergence

| Criterion | Visionary Excellence (+20 Pts) | Architectural Slop (-10 Pts) | Expert Analysis (Anara's Insight) |
|:----------|:-------------------------------|:-----------------------------|:-----------------------------------|
| Bus Architecture | Dedicated buses for Music/SFX/Voice with real-time FX. | All sounds on the 'Master' bus with no hierarchy. | Audio is the invisible landscape. |
| 3D Attenuation | Custom curves and distance-based LP filters. | Flat 2D sound for spatial actors. | Presence is the result of perspective. |
| Polyphony Logic | Strict sample-limiters and priority-voice nodes. | Sound-stacking causing ear-fatigue and clipping. | Silence is as important as sound. |


### 🌟 Visionary's Blueprint
Evaluate the 'Immersion'. Use the Godot AudioServer to its full potential.
