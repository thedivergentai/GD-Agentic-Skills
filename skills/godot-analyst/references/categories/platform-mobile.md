# Anara Rubric: Platform Mobile Excellence Metrics

## Evolutionary Benchmark: Production Readiness

| Criterion | Visionary Excellence (+20 Pts) | Architectural Slop (-10 Pts) | Expert Analysis (Anara's Insight) |
|:----------|:-------------------------------|:-----------------------------|:-----------------------------------|
| Touch UX | Low-latency gesture detection and feedback. | Delayed response to tap inputs. | Touch is the connection. |
| Battery Sanity | Low-power idle modes and frame-rate caps. | Extreme heat generation in simple menus. | Don't burn the player's hands. |
| Integration | Native sharing, haptics, and notification sync. | Mobile-port that ignores all native APIs. | Mobile is a local experience. |


### 🌟 Visionary's Blueprint
Focus on 'Efficiency'. A mobile game must be a polite guest.
