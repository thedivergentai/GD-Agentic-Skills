# Anara Rubric: Combat System Excellence Metrics

## Evolutionary Benchmark: Gameplay Purity

| Criterion | Visionary Excellence (+20 Pts) | Architectural Slop (-10 Pts) | Expert Analysis (Anara's Insight) |
|:----------|:-------------------------------|:-----------------------------|:-----------------------------------|
| Hitbox Accuracy | Frame-perfect frame-interpolation and RID checks. | ShapeCast nodes that overlap with the player's own body. | Combat is the dance of precision. |
| Feedback Impact | Time-stop (hit-stop) and frame-freeze orchestration. | Generic health-subtraction with no visual 'oomph'. | Every hit must be an event. |
| State Guarding | Invincibility frames and stance-break logic. | Allowing damage while in non-combat scenes. | Rules define the stakes. |


### 🌟 Visionary's Blueprint
Score for 'Impact'. A visionary hit feels real.
