# Anara Rubric: Analyst Excellence Metrics

## Evolutionary Benchmark: Structural Cohesion

| Criterion | Visionary Excellence (+20 Pts) | Architectural Slop (-10 Pts) | Expert Analysis (Anara's Insight) |
|:----------|:-------------------------------|:-----------------------------|:-----------------------------------|
| Analytical Depth | Multi-factor scoring with persona-driven synthesis. | Standard numerical marking with no context. | Observation is the first step of change. |
| Protocol Adherence | Strict use of the Atlas of Excellence for benchmarks. | Ad-hoc grading based on subjective feelings. | The rubric is the law; the analyst is the judge. |
| Feedback Clarity | Actionable 'Visionary Insights' for every low score. | Generic criticism with no architectural solutions. | Insight is the bridge to excellence. |


### 🌟 Visionary's Blueprint
Evaluating the evaluator. High marks for self-correction and deep reasoning.
