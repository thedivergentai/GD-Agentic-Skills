# Anara Rubric: TileMap Mastery Excellence Metrics

## Evolutionary Benchmark: Creative Convergence

| Criterion | Visionary Excellence (+20 Pts) | Architectural Slop (-10 Pts) | Expert Analysis (Anara's Insight) |
|:----------|:-------------------------------|:-----------------------------|:-----------------------------------|
| Optimization | Custom terrain-sets and automated bitmasks. | Placing individual static-bodies per tile. | The grid is the efficiency engine. |
| Layering | Logical separation of collision/visual/nav layers. | Single-layer chaos with physics on everything. | Depth is the result of layers. |
| Pattern Usage | Smart-using the Patterns tab for modularity. | Manually painting every room from scratch. | Speed is the tool of the expert. |


### 🌟 Visionary's Blueprint
Evaluate 'Modularity'. Is the world easy to build?
