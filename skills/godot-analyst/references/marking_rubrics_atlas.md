### 🏛️ Sector IX: Genre-Specific Performance Thresholds
*Technical benchmarks tailored to specific gameplay architectures.*

| Genre | Max Draw Calls | Physics Body Count | Script complexity (`_process`) | Expert Focus |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **RPG / Open World** | 400 - 600 | 100 - 300 | Medium (Streaming) | Resource loading & Threaded I/O |
| **FPS / Action** | 200 - 400 | 50 - 150 | Low (Input Latency) | Input buffering & Raycast batches |
| **RTS / Horde** | 100 - 200 | 1000+ (Servers) | High (Batching) | RenderingServer & MultiMesh |
| **Platformer / 2D**| 50 - 150 | 50 - 100 | Very Low | Pixel-perfect collisions & UI |
| **Simulation** | 300 - 500 | 500+ | High (Data-Oriented) | WorkerThreadPool & Typed Arrays |

**Validation Protocol**: Analysts must identify the primary genre before running the `score_modernity.py` script. The "Acceptable Slop" varies significantly between a pixel-art platformer and a 10,000-unit RTS.
